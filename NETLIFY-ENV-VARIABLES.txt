HOW TO ADD IN NETLIFY:

2. Select your site
3. Go to: Site configuration → Environment variables
4. Click "Add a variable"
5. For each variable above:

   - Enter the Value
   - Click "Create variable"
6. After adding all variables, click "Trigger deploy"
